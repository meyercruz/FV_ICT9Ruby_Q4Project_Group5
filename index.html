---- What's Next ----


As incoming grade 10 students, we naturally expect most subjects to become more challenging, yet still very beneficial for our growth. Specifically, for the subject I.C.T., we are looking forward to learning even deeper and complex coding, and for the subject to offer us even more advanced lessons that will help prepare us for future coding activities and assessments. We hope that next year's I.C.T. program will not only help us strengthen our current skills but also help us build and develop them even more and widen our knowledge when it comes to coding. We also look forward to being introduced to more programming concepts of technology which we can also apply in real-life. To this also includes more collaborative activities to offer more opportunities. All in all, we expect that grade 10's I.C.T. program will lead us towards having an even deeper understanding and greater knowledge in the digital world.
